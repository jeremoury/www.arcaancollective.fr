| About |


ARCAAN Collective

Aide et Recherche pour la Cration Artistique Audiovisuelle et Numrique

Initiate by Antoine Briot and Jeremy Oury, Collectif ARCAAN combines audio and visual skills to make singular mapping or digital installation.

We pursue explorations into intermedia forms to create synaesthesic and minimalist partition with unexpected narrations in various way (architectural mapping, immersive installation, fulldome, ...). We focus on a research about illusions on immersive forms in order to place the viewer at the center of a minimalist virtual universe to disrupt his perception of space.

IMMERSIVE was diffused in several digital festival (VISUALIA 2018 and 2019 Croatia, BAM 2018 Belgium, ICIA 2018 Poland, ISEA Symponium 2019 South Korea), in various fulldome festivals and won the public prize at ICIA 2018 and the "Best short fulldome form" award at the Macon Film Festival 2019.
